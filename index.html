football

Today Football is game played in an open field between two teams. ... In some forms of football the players are allowed to carry the ball in their hands and score a goal. Soccer is the most popular form of football, it has two teams of 11 players each who move the ball using only their head and feet.

Basketball

Basketball is a team sport in which two teams of five players try to score points by throwing or shooting a ball through the top of a basketball hoop while following a set of rules. Basketball is one of the world's most popular and widely viewed sports.

cricket

Cricket is a sport which is played between two teams of eleven players each who score runs (points). This is done by hitting the ball across the boundary, or by running between two sets of three small, wooden posts called wickets. The wickets are at each end of a rectangle of short grass called 'the pitch.
